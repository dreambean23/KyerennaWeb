My Raster Image

This image is inspired by little things that make up very big parts of me. Lets start with the background of the photo, is Smithspoint beach on Long Island. It's one of my favorite places in the world, I grew up going here and being that I'm from Long Island it's a testiment to my visceral need to be near a body of water. The music notes represent how much music means to me, not only am I musically inclined but bothh sides of my family are as well. Without music as an outlet I don't know what I'd do and that's how in earned a spot in my photo. Lastly is the sky, as pretty as it looks there is a deeper meaning. Those are the lesbian pride colors, I came out as a lesbian when I was 19 and living in my truth has truly saved my life in ways I can't begin to describe. Those colors mean so much to myself as well as millions of others before me but when I look at them I get filled with happiness and memories that I've made since coming out as an individual and with my long term girlfriend.
